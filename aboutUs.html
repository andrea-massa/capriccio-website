About Us

Our History

In the eighties, two cousins from a little town in Italy moved to the Unites states.

One of them went to New Jersey and the other to Connecticut, parting ways.

After 20 years of experience, they decided to partner up and open Capriccio here in Charlotte.

Ever since 2005 Capriccio has been providing its costumers with authentic Italian food.

Our Cuisine

Neapolitan Cuisine comes from the city of Naples in Southern Italy.

During the 80s, Neapolitan cuisine and Sicilian cuisine started becoming popular in New York.

New dishes started to emerge as well as the famous 'New York Style Pizza'.

We take those dishes and their original flavors from Italy to New York and from New York to Charlotte!
